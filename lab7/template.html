The Magic ColorBox Making-n-Moving Thingamajiggy

  • Add names of colors to the text box and click the "create boxes" button to create them
  • Feel free to click the "create boxes" more than once to make lots of them
  • Hover over a box to change the background color of the text box to match it
  • Click on a box to to select it
  • Use the buttons to move it right or left
  • Double-click to delete
